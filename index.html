User-Centered Product Person

Hello! I do UX/UI.

I'm a Carnegie Mellon University + Ironhack UX/UI Bootcamp grad with several years of experience in the tech industry. My work is driven by:

  • Problem-solving: Always curious, thinking up new solutions, and taking initiative. My favorite part of any job is investigating how things are currently done and finding ways to make it even better.

  • Data: I began my career as a data analyst, became certified in Google Analytics as an intern, independently learned SQL within a matter of days, and have a mild obsession with pivot tables. I continue to inform decision-making with real numbers as much as possible.

  • Collaboration: With my experience in IT project management, I have facilitated communication between cross-functional teams of varying technical abilities. I actually get along well with engineers!

  • Empathy: Through my years at various tech companies, I became intrigued by how people use products, and how I could make their experience more delightful. Having recently graduated from Ironhack's UX/UI bootcamp, I look forward to working on products that people use and love.
